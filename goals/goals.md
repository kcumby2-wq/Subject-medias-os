Review cadence: monthly

## Q3 2026

### GOAL 1 — Individual Athlete Walk-Up Volume
Target: 40+ packages sold per month (Photo $49 / Video $99 / Elite $139)
Metric: packages sold / month · package mix (Photo vs Video vs Elite) · SR upsell attach rate
Why: walk-up sales are the highest-frequency, lowest-friction revenue channel — every family on-site is a potential transaction, and every delivery is a confirmed SR upsell trigger (see Goal 6)

### GOAL 2 — Club Program Bookings
Target: 4–6 programs booked per month (JV $199 / Varsity $349 / Large Program $499 / Season $999)
Metric: programs contacted → responded → agreement sent → fee paid (GHL pipeline stages) / average tier per booking
Why: club programs are the recurring backbone — a Season tier booking ($999) is worth roughly 20+ walk-up Photo packages in one signature, and a strong program relationship compounds into next season's renewal

### GOAL 3 — Tournament Organization Bookings
Target: 2–3 tournament organizations booked per month (Local $499 / Regional $999 / Major $1,999)
Metric: tournament orgs contacted → agreement sent → event scheduled → event complete
Why: one signed tournament fee unlocks every team in the building for a weekend — the highest revenue-per-touch channel, and a strong on-site showing is the best lead generator for the Club Program funnel (coaches who see Subject Media at a tournament become program prospects)

### GOAL 4 — Pylon Outreach Activation
Target: 150+ of the 1,500-contact Pylon list moved past "Contacted" into "Responded" or further per month, starting with the 7v7 Football launch cohort
Metric: contacted / responded / agreement sent / signed, tracked in `data/outreach/outreach-log.md` — currently empty and the first thing this goal should fix
Why: the list is a built asset sitting idle — Goals 2 and 3 cannot scale past word-of-mouth until this pipeline is actually logged and worked session over session

### GOAL 5 — SubjectSkillz Creator Pipeline Activation
Target: 3–5 active creators onboarded and assigned to at least one event per month (Intro → Production → Live)
Metric: creators onboarded / creators assigned to an event / creators advanced a level
Why: the creator pipeline is what makes Subject Media's delivery model scale without Ky personally filming every event — every creator activated is direct capacity unlocked, and every Level 2–3 creator is a paid, portfolio-building opportunity SubjectSkillz exists to provide

### GOAL 6 — SubjectReport Cross-Sell Conversion
Target: 20% of delivered media packages convert to a $249 SR Player Transcript purchase
Metric: deliveries completed / SR upsell sent (auto-fires 48hrs post-delivery, confirmed live) / SR upsell converted
Why: this automation is already confirmed working — the goal now is making sure delivery volume (Goal 1) and conversion rate both get tracked, since an unmeasured automation can't be improved
