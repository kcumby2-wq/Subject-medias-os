# Subject Media — Teams Program

**Professional sports media for your program. On behalf of your athletes.**

## How It Works

Subject Media comes to your program. Your program pays a small access fee — that's it. We handle everything else. Our crew films your athletes. Parents and players can purchase their individual coverage packages directly from us at the event. Your athletes get professional media. Your program pays almost nothing.

We make our money selling directly to families who want their kid's footage. Your program is the platform that makes that possible.
